Control

Move: WSAD / Arrow
Jump: Space
Switch View: V

Movement

Character movement obeys preservation of momentum, meaning the controlling body may keep moving for a short distance when stopping.
It can either help you to jump for a longer distance, or let you fall from the edge.
You are also allowed to jump once while in the air.

Level

The game contains 2 levels.
The first level is mainly a jumping puzzle.
The second level tests your skill on how well you can control the character movement, and balance.

Penalty

The game features death.
Falling onto the surface from a very high altitude may kill your character, so be aware.
Once the character is dead, game will restart from the beginning of the current level.
